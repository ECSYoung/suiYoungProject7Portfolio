    About Me

    I'm...

    Sui Young! A front end developer based in the GTA! I graduated recently from Juno College of Technology. I have also taken self-paced courses through various online sources like Udemy. I hope to eventually graduate into full-stacked development, but only after I'm gained a strong amount of knowledge and experience in front end and the various languages that includes!

    I'm Passionate...

    About designing and creating creative, original, user friendly, socially aware, and accessibility content. I love learning, enhancing my knowledge, and the challenge of solving problems using logic, experience, and creativity! My favorite languages are React, jQuery, and SASS!

    I was...

    So many things before returning to code! I spent the last 7 years in the real estate industry performing various roles. I've worked in customer service, sales, design and administration. From these roles I've learned the importance of professionalism, team work, and structure. I've gained valuable experience in assessing priorities, setting goals and how to accurately and appropriately manage my time to achieve these goals. Most importantly, I've learned the importance of asking for help once I've exhausted my own resources!

    I Also Enjoy...

    Illustrating, cooking, D.I.Y., and going out to spend quality time with friends and loved ones. My favorite past-time is cooking in a group and trying new recipes.
